ARMADILLO

Protected Data Storage Solution

The Market's First EMP & CME Hardened Storage System

1. The Problem

  • Vulnerable Digital Infrastructure
    • Electromagnetic Pulses (EMPs)
    • Coronal Mass Ejections (CMEs)
    • Fire, water, and other physical disasters
  • Limited Accessible Solutions
    • The market lacks robust, disaster-proof systems
  • Heightened Threats
    • Solar cycle peaking in 2025 (CME risk)
    • Geopolitical tensions elevating EMP threats
    • Traditional disaster recovery is manual and slow

2. The Solution: Armadillo Platform

A disaster-resistant storage device built to safeguard digital assets against catastrophic threats:

  • Military-grade protection in a desktop-sized appliance
  • 100% data integrity maintained even with 75% drive failure
  • Always-on backup plus real-time data access
  • Automated offline archiving for immediate network restoration

  • Flagship product with enterprise-grade capacity
  • Full redundancy features

  • Mid-range solution, targeting SMEs
  • Same protection features in a smaller capacity
  • Retail Price: $14,950 (Year 1)

4. Unique Features

  • Proprietary Redundant RAID 6: 8-drive setup tolerating up to 6 drive failures
  • Real-Time Offline Storage Swapping: Automated transitions to offline, hardened backups
  • Custom Aluminum Shell: Electroplated with proprietary alloy for maximum conductivity/protection
  • Comprehensive Software Suite: Local NAS and remote LAN backup, extensible modules
  • Modular & User-Extensible: Sacrificial data/power modules, installable software modules
